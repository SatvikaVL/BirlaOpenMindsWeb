Campus Life

School Library

Digital Library available for middle and senior years

"Something magical happens when you read a book" J.K Rowling. Students are encouraged to browse , research , find out analyse and synthesise and use the library facility to the maximum. Reading corner as a Learning Center is a must in the Pre-School classes. Primary classes are also encouraged to have a class library where students shares weekly one book. The Primary library is adequately equipped with age appropriate stock of books of all genres and different periodicals for subject enrichment.

Lab Facilities

All labs have all the equipment needed to learn efficiently

To boost the meta-cognitive abilities of the students and cater to the experiential aspect of the curriculum requirement , the school's infrastructure includes full fledged and separate Biology, Physics , Chemistry , IT ,ILS and Math Labs. These labs allow students to rethink, rework and solve problems at their own learning pace.

Birla Smart Schooling (Only for 9-12 graders)

Help students to understand the topic

2D and 3D animated videos and simulations that cover topics related to Science and Mathematics. We have a reserve of more than 400 2D and 3D animated videos. The 3D animated videos particularly act as powerful visualization tools for easy understanding of complex concepts. These resources make learning and studying a far more creative, dynamic and fun experience for BOMISites.
